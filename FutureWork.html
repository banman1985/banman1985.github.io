Charitable Organizations

I have a passion for helping charities reach both potential donors as well as people in need. To this end, while I am willing to design websites for a broad array of organizations, I am especially motivated to design websites for certain charities. I offer discounts to qualifying organizations. To qualify, your charity must meet the following requirements:

  • It must be a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization.
  • A minimum of a three-star rating on Charity Navigator, if listed on that website.
  • A minimum a B+ rating on Charity Watch, if rated on that website.
  • I will neither provide a discount, nor work with in any capacity, groups with discriminatory practices, or that have a history of contributing to the oppression of any group of people.
  • All charities that meet the above qualifications are still subject to final approval by me.

While the above limitations may seem harsh, I trust that you understand that providing a charitable discount as well as designing a website is providing personal support to an organization. Because of this, I will be as demanding of the charities that I support with my work as I am with the ones I donate to financially.

Businesses and Non-Charitable Organizations

From mom-and-pop owned antique stores, to amateur soccer teams, to multi-state trucking companies with hundreds of employees, I can design your website! Many website developers set minimums: minimums that might be your entire budget. You won't find that with me. Why? Because not every website needs to be cutting-edge. Your website should be like you, like your business: unique.

If you own a small pottery store and you want to showcase new pieces, then your site probably needs to handle plenty of pictures but not that much traffic. If you own a doughnut shop that only handles large corporate orders, then you pretty much only need a home page, a menu, and an order form. Contact me today, and we can find out what unique website I can design for you, within your budget.

Website Renovations

In addition to designing new websites from the ground up, I also offer website renovation. Website renovation might be right for you if your website is HTML5 compatible and if the desired changes are mostly or completely cosmetic (colors, font, etc), or are minor layout adjustments. Please contact me to schedule and estimate. Renovations can usually be done in as little as one day, and can be often be scheduled sooner than full website designs.
