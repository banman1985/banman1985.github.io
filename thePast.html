Methuselah Foundation

The Methuselah Foundation is a bio-medical non-profit focused on sponsoring life extension research and technology, including researching rejection-free organ transplants and sponsoring studies into the genetic components of aging. The Foundation contacted me about a new website design and in 2022, the new Methuselah Foundation website launched.
