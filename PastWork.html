Methuselah Foundation

The Methuselah Foundation is a bio-medical non-profit focused on sponsoring life extension research and technology, including researching rejection-free organ transplants and sponsoring studies into the genetic components of aging. The Foundation contacted me about a new website design and in 2024, the new Methuselah Foundation website launched.

The Humane Society of Missouri is a wonderful charity that rescues abused and neglected pets, work, and farm animals. Through legislation, spay/neuter programs, and active investigation, the HSMO helps protect the most vulnerable of creatures. In 2023 I proudly joined their mission by helping design their new website.

Raw Steel is a CrossFit gym (or box) owned by nurses Mel and Jacienda Farrales. Raw Steel proudly serves the fitness needs of Springfield and southwest Missouri. It also provides a caring and competitive community for new and experienced athletes in a family-friendly environment. In addition to being a member, I designed their new website in 2022.
